About us

Bubbles is a handmade soap company established by couple Saba and Maha. Their mission is to design products, that become must have, can’t live without favorites. Perfecting completely natural, healing recipes that smell amazing, really work & are beautifully plastic-free packaged.Bath bombs and Bubblebath are their specialities and are specially designed for kids. They use only the best essential oils, botanicals, and minerals including organic rosemary leaf, organic lavender flowers, organic cocoa butter, Shea butter and many more.

As soon as they started selling the soaps they could see that they had created a product that people wanted and needed and that there was a demand for their soaps. The key was to create the products at a price everyone could afford.The determination to barter, take a risk and deliver on honest handshakes means the business has self-funded its own growth.

We’re a company that is open and transparent, and we’ve created a brand that showcases this, as well as our roots. It’s a brand with personality that has genuine heritage and in today’s world this can go a long way to helping build a loyal customer base.
