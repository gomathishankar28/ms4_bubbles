About Us

Our Story

Bubbles is an Indian based company established by couple Sab and Maha Their mission is to design products, that become must have, can’t live without favorites. Perfecting completely natural, healing recipes that smell amazing, really work & are beautifully plastic-free packaged.

Bath bombs and Bubblebath are their specialities and are specially designed for kids. We use only the best essential oils, botanicals, and minerals including organic rosemary leaf, organic lavender flowers, organic cocoa butter, Shea butter and many more.

As soon as We started selling the soaps We could see that we had created a product that people wanted and needed and that there was a demand for my soaps. The key was to create the products at a price everyone could afford.

The determination to barter, take a risk and deliver on honest handshakes means the business has self-funded its own growth. “We were very fortunate that the first few factories we worked with knew we had deals with the major supermarkets and agreed I could pay them when I got paid.

We’re a company that is open and transparent, and we’ve created a brand that showcases this, as well as our roots. It’s a brand with personality that has genuine heritage and in today’s world this can go a long way to helping build a loyal customer base
